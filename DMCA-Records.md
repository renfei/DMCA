### Legend

- **Web page / Article** → Direct content on renfei.net → removed or 410 Gone
- **BitTorrent Tracker** → Only info hash appears on tracker.renfei.net → hash permanently banned
- **Status**:

    - `Invalid` → Notice did not meet DMCA requirements
    - `Counter-Notice Received` → Content restored after valid counter-notice
